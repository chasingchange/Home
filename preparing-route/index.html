Taco Beef Meal Prep Bowl

Macros | 1 Serving (approx)

  • Calories: 510
  • Protein: 44 g
  • Carbs: 32 g
  • Fats: 24 g

(Assumes lean beef + Greek-yogurt crema)

Ingredients | 1 Serving

  • 93% lean ground beef, 5 oz (140 g)
  • Romaine lettuce, chopped, 2 cups (90 g)
  • Cherry tomatoes, 75 g
  • Black beans, 60 g
  • Shredded Mexican cheese blend, 28 g
  • Avocado, 40 g
  • Crushed tortilla chips, 15 g
  • Salsa, 60 g
  • Nonfat Greek yogurt, 60 g
  • Taco seasoning, 5 g

Ingredients | 7 Servings

  • 93% lean ground beef, 35 oz (980 g)
  • Romaine lettuce, 630 g
  • Cherry tomatoes, 525 g
  • Black beans, 420 g
  • Shredded Mexican cheese blend, 196 g
  • Avocado, 280 g
  • Crushed tortilla chips, 105 g
  • Salsa, 420 g
  • Nonfat Greek yogurt, 420 g
  • Taco seasoning, 35 g

Instructions

  1. Brown ground beef in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add taco seasoning and a splash of water; simmer 3–4 minutes.
  3. Let beef cool slightly for meal prep storage.
  4. Layer lettuce into containers.
  5. Add tomatoes, beans, cheese, avocado, and salsa.
  6. Top with beef.
  7. Mix Greek yogurt with extra salsa if desired for a creamy dressing.
  8. Add tortilla chips right before eating to keep crunch.
