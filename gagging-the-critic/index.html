Gagging the Critic

How to quiet the voice in your head long enough to think clearly.

When your brain starts spiraling, the goal is not to win an argument with yourself. The goal is to slow the thought down, separate fact from feeling, and respond with grounded clarity.

● Ground yourself: breathe, slow down, answer honestly, don’t rush this page.

5-Step Reflection

Step 1

Write the thought exactly how it sounds in your head. Don’t clean it up.

Step 2

Are you basing the thought on fact or feeling?

Sometimes a thought feels true before it is true. Name what is actually happening.

Fact or feeling

Step 3

Let the fear speak plainly. Don’t dramatize it more than necessary, but don’t avoid it either.

Step 4

Now answer like an adult, not a panic spiral. What is the more realistic outcome?

Step 5

Step outside yourself. If a friend came to you with this exact thought, what would you say?

Perspective Shift

Start with Step 1 and Step 4 for the fastest clarity reset.
